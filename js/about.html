About Chronicles of Power

An open-source digital archive dedicated to rigorous history at the intersection of political power, civil conflict, and mineral resources.

Our Mission

History is too often told in silos. Yet the great dramas of nations — the rise and fall of powers, the ignition of civil wars, the carving of borders — are almost always entangled with the control of valuable minerals and resources.

Chronicles of Power exists to tell those connected stories with clarity, depth, and honesty.

What We Cover

  • Politics — How states and regimes have used resource wealth to consolidate power and fund armies.
  • Civil Wars — Internal conflicts where the prize is control of mines, oil fields, and strategic minerals.
  • Minerals — Diamonds, cobalt, coltan, oil, iron, rare earths — and the human systems that form around them.

Completely Open Source on GitHub

This entire project lives in a public GitHub repository. All code and article text is version-controlled. Anyone can propose corrections, additions, or new articles via pull requests. The site is hosted for free on GitHub Pages.

How to Contribute

  1. Fork the repository on GitHub
  2. Create a new branch for your article or fix
  3. Write your piece following the existing article template
  4. Open a pull request with a clear description and sources
